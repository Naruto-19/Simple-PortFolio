About Me


I am a passionate fine arts graduate from the University of Development Alternatives in Dhaka, Bangladesh, specializing in painting and drawing. Throughout my four years of study, I immersed myself in the fundamentals of artistic principles, explored various art styles, delved into art history, and honed my craft through rigorous practice.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I have also established myself as a versatile freelance graphic designer, bringing my creative vision to various projects and collaborations. With a strong foundation in both traditional and digital art forms, I am dedicated to pushing the boundaries of creativity and delivering impactful visual experiences. Explore my portfolio to see the fruits of my artistic journey and the diversity of my work.
